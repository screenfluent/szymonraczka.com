# szymonraczka.com

Personal website for Szymon Raczka, written primarily in English. It combines a homepage, posts, notes, and projects. A Polish version may exist later, but English is the default language for now.

## Language

**Homepage**:
The public entry page at `/`. It introduces Szymon and lists selected Projects and Posts.

**Project**:
A public item representing something Szymon built, is building, or wants to present as part of his work. A Project has a title and a month/year label.

**Page**:
A standalone public route, such as `/about` or `/now`. Pages are not part of the post stream.

**Post**:
A published text by Szymon. Posts may be personal, reflective, technical, or process-oriented.

**Bespoke Page**:
A Page with its own presentation or interaction, not constrained to the default page layout.

**Bespoke Post**:
A Post with its own presentation or interaction, not constrained to the default article layout. Bespoke Posts may be used for interactive storytelling.

**Bespoke**:
A presentation mode for a Page or Post that gives it its own designed experience instead of using the default layout.
